About

Haute Ecole is devoted to promoting the regal horse breeds found in and around the Iberian Penninsula: Andalusions, Friesians, Lipizzaners, Lusitanos, and Spanish-Norman Horses.

You do not need to own any of the breeds accepted by the group in order to join. Everyone is welcome!

Haute Ecole features regular shows as well as club-exclusive prizes and awards for those who register their horses, enter shows, or participate in other club activites.

Keep your eyes peeled for future events!

Spotlight

Did you know?

You can request club shows, events, and chat parties! Any club member is welcome to submit their request. Just send a message in the ongoing request thread on the club message boards!

Given the low amount of participation in this club, your request is guaranteed to be fulfilled. Club shows are a great way to increase your horse's points, as they award much more points than the dailies!

Show Series

Outdated

HEI runs shows every Friday and Saturday. Show grades are set for certain dates, and members are invited to request classes for those dates by heading over to the club Message Boards. If no class has been requested for a grade, then a random class will be selected.

Monthly Prizes

Everyone who enters HEI shows is eligible to win additional prizes at the end of the month. Members only need to enter one show a month to qualify for monthly prizes, but members who enter shows more often have a higher chance at winning prizes. Prizes can be anything from money, horses, and other unique awards. Winners will be notified directly. Members who enter a show that doesn't end up getting enough entries to run are still eligable for monthly prizes.
