About Me

Hello! I am the Duchess of Canterbury, and I am the manager of the Royal Stables of Amicran. I recently started playing WOS again after a hiatus of 6 years! This means I have to start from scratch again, but every end leads to new beginnings, right? I am hoping to specialize in Lippizaners and Arabians, and I am working on acquiring breeding facilities soon. In the meanwhile, I show the horses in a variety of disciplines. My inbox is always open, so feel free to message me about anything!

Stable Shows

I do stable shows occasionally and upon request! Currently I am only able to host halter shows, but as I grow I will expand my facilities! However, I do not have the means to promise a large winning purse.

Fillers

I have a second account where I take in/buy unwanted horses and use them as fillers. As long as they are healthy, I do not care what breed/circuit they are! You can find my filler account at the button below!

Graphic Services

I am willing to make layouts for very reasonable prices! I am a certified front-end developer with experience making many types of pages. If you want to see more examples of my work, visit my website below!
