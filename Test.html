Breeding Info

I offer breeding services and I am willing to breed some of my horses! However, I have a few rules governing the horses I am willing to breed with my own. These rules really only apply to Lipizzaners and Arabians, and I am much more lax with other breeds as I do not plan to create lines with them. Although, no matter what breed your horse is, please message me before breeding!

Your horse must be...

  • at least 22 y/o!
  • within ~100 points of my horse
  • the same breed as my horse
  • SC of at least 3 shows
  • of the same circuit level (C, B, A, etc.)
  • of good health and up to date on all vet and farrier procedures

I reserve the right to withold breedings for any reason!
Please message me before breeding!
