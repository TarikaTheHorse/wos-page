Want to know what the lesson comments mean and how to adjust your rider settings for your horse? Look no further!

Do be aware that the order of rider settings changes depending on where it is!

In rider settings, the order is Mouth, Speed, Reins, Crop, Arms, Legs, Post, and Stride, while in the training riding settings the order is Mouth, Speed, Reins, Stride, Crop, Arms, Legs, and Post.

Category# Option | Lesson Comments
Mouth
  1. Likes where the bit is in their mouth
  2. Are slightly annoyed with the bit in their mouth
  3. Do not like the way the bit is in their mouth
  4. Absolutely hates the way the bit is in their mouth
Speed
  1. They need to maintain this speed.
  2. They need to slightly slow down or speed up.
  3. They need to slow down or speed up.
  4. They need to really slow down or really speed up.
  5. They need to change their speed before they run the horse into the ground.
Reins
  1. The horse is behaving well with the reins the way they are now.
  2. The horse is pulling slightly at the reins they way they are now.
  3. The horse does not like the reins the way they are now.
  4. The horse absolutely hates the reins the way they are now.
Crop
  1. This member is properly using their crop.
  2. This member is slightly misusing their crop.
  3. This member is misusing their crop.
  4. This member is badly misusing their crop.
  5. This member is abusing their horse with their crop.
Arms
  1. Their arms look perfect.
  2. Their arms look good but could be better.
  3. Their arms are only okay. They need to change what they're doing.
  4. Their arms are going to freak this horse out! What are they doing now?
Legs
  1. Their legs look fine. *(or legs look excellent)
  2. Their legs look good but could be better.
  3. Their legs are only okay.
  4. Their legs look odd. They really need to change something.
Post
  1. Their posting is not needed for a halter show. *or posting exactly as they should be.
  2. The posting is a little too much or they need to post a little more.
  3. The posting is all over the place. They need to change what they're doing.
  4. The posting looks horrible. They need to stop it before they fall off.
  5. Their posting is just all wrong, something needs to be fixed.
Stride
  1. Keep their gait where it is.
  2. Change their gait just a little bit.
  3. They really need to change their gait.
  4. Change their gait completely.
