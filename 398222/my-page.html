About Me

Hello! I am the Duchess of Canterbury, and I am the manager of the Royal Stables of Amicran. I recently started playing WOS again after a hiatus of 6 years! This means I have to start from scratch again, but every end leads to new beginnings, right? I am hoping to specialize in Lipizzaners and Arabians and create lines with them. In the meanwhile, I am always tweaking my layout ever so slightly! My inbox is always open, so feel free to message me about anything!

Stable Shows

I do stable shows occasionally and upon request! Currently I am only able to host halter and dressage shows, but as I grow I will expand my facilities! I also want help run shows for my club, Haute Ecole - Iberian, in the future!

Fillers

I have a second account where I take in/buy unwanted horses and use them as fillers. As long as they are healthy, I do not care what breed/circuit they are! You can find my filler account at the button below!

Graphic Services

I am willing to make layouts for very reasonable prices! I am a certified front-end developer with experience making many types of pages. If you want to see more examples of my work, visit my website below!

Breedings

I offer breeding services and I am willing to breed some of my horses! However, I have a few rules governing the horses I am willing to breed with my own. These rules really only apply to Lipizzaners and Arabians, and I am much more lax with other breeds as I do not plan to create lines with them. Although, no matter what breed your horse is, please message me before breeding!

Your horse must be...

  • at least 22 y/o!
  • within ~100 points of my horse
  • the same breed as my horse
  • SC of at least 3 shows
  • of the same circuit level (C, B, A, etc.)
  • of good health and up to date on all vet and farrier procedures

I reserve the right to withold breedings for any reason!
Please message me before breeding!
